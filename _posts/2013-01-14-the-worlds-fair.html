An identity for a one day event held at HarperCollins by the International division. The paper aeroplanes communicate the idea that HarperCollins books are travelling across the globe for sale in international territories.

The identity included banners, posters, a passport, keynote presentation and bunting. I also designed the masthead for The Stringer the division’s newsletter.
