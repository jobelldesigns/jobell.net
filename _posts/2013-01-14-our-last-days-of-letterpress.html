Our Last Days of Letterpress is a set of nine posters proclaiming that time is running out. The posters are designed to highlight the value of letterpress technology and encourage workshops to thrive.

The process of printing each poster was a labour of love; each poster design had to run through the press a minimum of three times, each poster was printed in a batch of 30. Ink was mixed by hand, countless cans of white spirit were used. There are no carbon copies, all of which makes them special.
