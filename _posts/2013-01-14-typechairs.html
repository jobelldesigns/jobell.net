A conceptual project pushing the boundaries of a type specimen.

Each chair has been modified to represent a specific typeface:

  1. Archetype Renner Chair - extra legs welded to increase chairs descenders
  2. Futura Black - middle sawn and removed
  3. Beo Sans - legs scrambled with blow torch
  4. Bell Centennial - counter shape removed with dipped corners to allow ink fill in
