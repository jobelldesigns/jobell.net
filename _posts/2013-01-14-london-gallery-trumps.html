Produced with artist duo Turnhurst, Gallery Trumps provides statistics on London galleries to spark discussion & debate about what makes an art gallery a success.

With over 20 galleries reviewed the pack also serves as a valuable learning resource with map and listings of the gallery locations.

London Gallery Trumps was featured in Art Review Magazine, The Art Newspaper and is available to buy in bookshops and galleries across London.
