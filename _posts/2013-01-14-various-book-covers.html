Yoga Series

HarperCollins Publishers

Cover Design & Branding

A re-branding of the esteemed Yoga guru B.K.S Iyengar’s range of books. The covers evoke a feeling of Indian culture but with a modern graphic edge.

Wonders of Life

HarperCollins Publishers

Cover Design

A book to accompany Brian Cox’s ground breaking TV series. Wonders of Life introduces the subject in its many forms from the lifecycle of a monarch butterfly to the smallest microbe on Earth to the biggest galaxy. The cover takes the richness of life and places Brian within it.

Billy

HarperCollins Publishers

Cover Re-Design

To mark the 10th anniversary of Billy, Pamela Stephenson’s highly successful biography, HarperCollins relaunched the title. For this I designed the lively cover and reverses.

The typeface is Blackout which is available free from theleagueofmoveabletype.com
