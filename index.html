Alternative Type Specimen

Project with Charlie Crook & Elishea Nicholson

The unexpected use of chairs for a type specimen; illustrating the key features of each typeface.

Our Last Days of Letterpress

Project with Laura Bowman & Grace Coughlan

A series of posters communicating the urgency for students to use Letterpress facilities in their limited time at university to learn about typography and printing. The posters are delivered directly to universities to advertise the opportunities of letterpress and the tube contains advice on where presses are available.

The Rio

The RIO cinema in Dalston is one of the few remaining independent cinemas in London. The re-branding establishes RIO’s uniqueness as an independent and its ability to offer a wider range of films. The new identity draws on RIO’s history and reflects the ‘electric’ era of the cinemas heyday.

Look Book

The Surplus re-use Network

RSA winning project with Sarah Brenkley & Hannah Rödde

‘Surplus’ is a network designed for the construction industry, aiming to reduce waste by making excess building materials available from large sites, to small sites for free. The network encourages re-use and benefits large sites from reduced landfill charges and positive CSR.

Scale in Advertising

Project with Tara Woolnough

Advertising can bend the truth. Making a humorous example of ‘Mini’ and ‘Big Mac’ the products true size is exposed in the ad space the brand predominantly uses.

Disposed of Cameras

An experimental process in which cameras, facing mirrors, recorded themselves being set on fire and burned. The unpredictable results document the true disposal of a disposable camera and highlight the beauty in destruction. A short film of the work was displayed on a burnt television in the fireplace of the late Paul Arden’s gallery.
