About

Having graduated with a first from Kingston University in 2009, I have gained experience in a range of great design companies. Working on branding projects, book design, print and illustration for clients including Royal Mail, Arts Council England and Limoncello Gallery.

Since 2010 I have been a freelance designer working for NB Studio, MARC&ANNA, HarperCollins and Octopus.

In 2012 I completed a short course in portraiture at Central Saint Martins to gain key skills in illustration and painting.
